Your tenants all have a location they want to reach. It is indicated by a thought bubble over their head.
You can change the layout of your rooms and corridors using the arrows on the side.
They are happy, when they can find a way to get to that location and will move there on their own.
Otherwise, they will become unhappy and the overall mood (upper left corner) goes down.
If they cannot get to their goal, they will stroll around.
If the overall mood gets to zero, you will be demolished!
Sometimes your tenants are especially impatient, watch out for the timer on the top right.
If the time runs out before they reach their destination, again, demolished!
